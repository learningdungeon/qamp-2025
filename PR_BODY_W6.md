## Summary
Tier 1 Week 6 introduces a story-based pathfinding game, "Quantum Courier". Students choose and compare routes on a small city map using noise numbers, then explain why one route is calmer. This mirrors the high-level idea behind QAOA in a middle-school-friendly way.
