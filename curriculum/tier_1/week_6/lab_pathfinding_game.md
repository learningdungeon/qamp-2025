# Lab — Quantum Courier Pathfinding Steps

Part A — Meet the city

1. Find Start and Goal on your map.
2. Each road has a small number: 1, 2, or 3.
   - 1 = almost smooth
   - 2 = a bit bumpy
   - 3 = very bumpy

Part B — Guess Route

1. With your partner, pick your Guess Route from Start to Goal.
2. Write down each road you use and its number.
3. Add the numbers to get your total noise score.

Part C — Route Idea cards

1. Your teacher gives your team two Route Idea cards.
2. For each card:
   - Trace the route on the map.
   - Add the numbers.
   - Record the total noise score.

Part D — Choose your champion

1. Compare all the scores.
2. Circle the route with the smallest total noise.
3. On your worksheet, finish this sentence:

   "Our calmest route was __________ because __________."

Part E — Optional challenge

Try to invent a new route with the same or lower score than your champion.
If you find one, explain how you noticed it.
